Great developer resources

Last updated: 19 December 2019

 

There are so many resources available to developers. There is only enough time for exploring the most relevant and impactful. Below is a list of some of the most useful and thought-provoking I've explored.

 

Coding practise

 

  • Code Complete by Steve McConnell

  • The Pragmatic Programmer by Andrew Hunt and David Thomas

 

Methods

 

  • Bridging the Communication Gap by Gojko Adzic

  • REWORK by Jason Fried

 

Business

 

  • Eric Sink on the Business of Software (Expert's Voice)

  • The Cathedral & the Bazaar: Musings on Linux and Open Source by an Accidental Revolutionary by Eric S. Raymond

  • The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses by Eric Ries

 

Technology stacks

 If you are looking for ways to get a rapid overview of technologies and stacks.

  • The State of ... surveys

    The state of JavaScript 2019 - a great overview of what's current in JavaScript and other web technologies.

  • Stack Share

    Stackshare - sometimes to be taken with a pinch of salt but still useful when looking up new technologies.

 

Career

 

  • Career and Survival Strategies for Software Developers by Dan Appleman

  • Crucial Conversations: Tools for Talking When Stakes Are High by Patterson, Grenny, McMillan and Switzler

 

Time management

 

  • Time management lecture by Randy Pausch (video and transcript)

    Randy Pausch Lecture: Time Management on YouTube

    Transcript of lecture (pdf)

  • Getting Things Done: The Art of Stress-Free Productivity by David Allen
